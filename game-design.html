Game Designer | Unreal Engine 5

    Currently bringing together my artistic & science technology skills in Unreal Engine 5 with a game demo targeted to the Steamdeck OLED. The game explores the themes surrounding souls, death & consciousness.

    I am incredibly passionate about the continued evolution of story telling & giving players new fun experiences...

    • #Tech-Artist

      Death Particles

      Excited by the possibility of using fire & explosions instead of blood spatters, I created this 'fire, smoke & sparks' effect using Niagra particles. It is applied to the player's mesh at the death event.

      Also tuned the ragdoll effect to make the bones seperate more, added a camera zoom back & delay before destroying the actor. This means you can watch as the ragdoll gets flung around. Then implemented an instant random player respawn in blueprint.

      • Output

        Death Particle Animation + Player reSpawn.

      • Skills

        Niagra + Blueprints.

    • #Design & #Tech-artist

      Piece of my Soul

      This is an experiment using audio generated particles to decorate a spirit world. It started off in a dark environment but this made navigation too hard. I then changed to having the particles being emitted in the player's viewport.

      Added emissive materials to make birds glow + a distotion FX on wings. Colours chosen to pop on OLED display. 200,000 particles being generated & shaped by the soundtrack.

      Gameplay will be a series of rings to fly through or pop similar to hogwarts legacy baloon mini game. This then unlocks the elephant in the main world.

      • Output

        Distortion FX on wings + Audio Pattern FX around Bird + Opening Level Sequence.

      • Skills

        Niagra + Sequencer + Blueprints.

    • #Design

      Character Demo

      Using polycam to 3D Scan this brilliant 'skull-angel' character I have adorning my fridge. Then imported into blender & accurig to convert to a skeletal mesh. Then to mixamo to tune some animations. Created animation blueprints & blendspaces to control animations.

      Deliberatley customising some more quirty animations to exagerate the features. For example a funny walk with hands all over the palce. The idle animation is praying... In the future I will go back to rig & animate the wings once I know how I'd like them to function.

      • Output

        Character Mesh + Skeletal Mesh + Animations + Blendspace + Animation Blueprint

      • Skills

        Polycam + Blender + Rigging + Blueprints
